6-Minute Walk Access to AC Transit Bus Stops in Berkeley

This map shows 6-minute (500 m) walking coverage around all AC Transit bus stops in Berkeley, calculated using network-based distances along the pedestrian street network (OSMnx). Blue areas indicate locations within an accessible walking distance of at least one bus stop, black points represent bus stop locations, and the city boundary is shown for reference. Together, these layers provide a baseline view of potential transit accessibility across Berkeley.

Data sources: AC Transit (bus stops), City of Berkeley (land boundary), U.S. Census Bureau (census tracts used for spatial filtering)
